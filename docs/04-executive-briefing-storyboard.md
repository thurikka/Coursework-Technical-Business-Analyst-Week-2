# Executive Briefing Storyboard

Use this as a starter for your PowerPoint or slide deck.

## Slide 1 - Recommendation

Write the answer first.

Example structure:
> Approve a focused Phase 1 Smart-Recovery portal that digitises simple self-serve debt recovery journeys while routing unsupported cases to agents with full context.

## Slide 2 - Why the problem matters now

Cover:
- manual operational waste
- scale of delinquent accounts
- revenue leakage or missed opportunities

## Slide 3 - Discovery evidence

Summarise:
- top pain points
- priority JTBD themes
- why the current workflow fails at scale

## Slide 4 - Value case

Show:
- top ranked opportunities
- 12-month ROI logic
- confidence or scenario notes

## Slide 5 - Phase 1 scope and exclusions

Be explicit about:
- what is in scope
- what is out of scope
- why this is the right-sized first release

## Slide 6 - To-Be workflow

Show:
- core customer journey
- major exception path
- routing and reporting points

## Slide 7 - Delivery readiness

Cover:
- backlog size and structure
- example stories
- prototype readiness
- key dependencies

## Slide 8 - Change risks and mitigations

Use ADKAR-driven points such as:
- agent trust
- manager visibility
- reporting and reinforcement needs

## Slide 9 - Approval ask

End with a specific next step.

Examples:
- approve Phase 1 funding
- approve pilot mobilisation
- approve backlog refinement and delivery planning

## Final review questions

- Is the recommendation obvious by slide 1?
- Are the numbers challenge-ready?
- Have you addressed operational objections honestly?
- Does the final ask sound specific enough to act on?
