# Submissions folder

Place your finished Week 2 artefacts here.

## Expected outputs

- `phase-1-scope-statement.md` or `.docx`
- `adkar-assessment.xlsx` or `.csv`
- `prioritised-jira-backlog.xlsx` or exported `.csv`
- `smart-recovery-wireframe-link.md` or prototype export
- `executive-briefing.pptx` or slide storyboard markdown
- `traceability-summary.md`

## Suggested review order

1. Scope statement
2. ADKAR analysis
3. Backlog
4. Wireframe
5. Executive briefing

## Final quality checklist

Before Week 2 is complete, confirm that:
- your Phase 1 scope includes clear exclusions
- backlog priorities reflect value, dependency, and adoption logic
- acceptance criteria describe outcomes, not implementation tasks
- the wireframe includes at least one exception path
- your briefing opens with a recommendation and ends with a specific ask
- you can trace at least one pain point through to a story and screen
