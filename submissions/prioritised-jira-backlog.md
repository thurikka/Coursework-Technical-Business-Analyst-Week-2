## Prioritisation Rationale

The backlog is prioritised to align with both the To-Be workflow and the value case. Foundational capabilities such as authentication and action logging are delivered first to enable secure access and data capture. Core account visibility and repayment actions follow, as these directly drive the primary ROI objective of improved recovery rates and reduced inbound contact. Exception handling is prioritised next to ensure failed self-service journeys are effectively routed and resolved. Supporting features such as reporting and account recovery are included to enhance operational visibility and resilience but are sequenced after core value delivery. This approach ensures that high-impact capabilities are delivered early while respecting critical dependencies and minimising change risk.
