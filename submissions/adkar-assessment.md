## ADKAR Assessment (Legacy-Trust Bank)

| stakeholder_group | awareness | desire | knowledge | ability | reinforcement | key_risk | mitigation_action | delivery_implication |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Agents** | Aware tools are fragmented (spreadsheets, emails, legacy DB) | Want easier work but fear added admin or poor handoffs | Limited understanding of new self-service and automated workflows| Limited by manual systems + reconciliation | Needs feedback on workload reduction | Reject portal if it adds effort or loses context | Provide unified account view + clear worklists + context-rich handoffs | Phase 1 backlog: agent worklist + account context + workload reporting |
| **Team leaders and managers** | Aware of missed follow-ups + low operational visibility | Want control + visibility | Unclear how automated workflows affect reporting and visibility | Dependent on manual reporting and reconciliation | Need visibility of workload and follow-up performance to sustain adoption | Portal seen as ineffective without proof of reduced workload | Real-time dashboard (follow-ups, workload, cases resolved via self-service) | Phase 1: reporting/dashboard requirement |
| **Operations leadership** | Aware of ~15% revenue loss + process inefficiency | Strong desire for automation, risk of overpromise concern | Needs clear traceability to ROI | Dependent on realistic implementation | Expect measurable outcomes post-launch | Loss of trust if benefits aren’t measurable | Track admin effort, follow-ups, recovery metrics | Executive requirement: ROI model + outcome tracking |
| **Finance** | Aware of revenue leakage | Wants credible, defensible value case | Strong financial expertise | Dependent on data quality | No continuous validation loop | Rejects weak or untestable assumptions | Transparent assumptions| Reporting: financial model + validation scenarios |
| **Product and delivery** | Aware requirements are unclear | Wants buildable, traceable scope | Strong delivery knowledge | Blocked by vague inputs | Needs clear success criteria | Backlog churn from unclear scope | Break into components (portal, worklist, automation, reporting) | Backlog: structured, traceable epics |
