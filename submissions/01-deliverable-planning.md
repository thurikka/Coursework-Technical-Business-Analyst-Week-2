| **4. Executive briefing deck (Pyramid Principle: scope, value, risks)** | Day 5 | 3-5 hours | - Inconsistent narrative<br>- Poor translation to business value<br>- Conflicting stakeholder priorities | - Communicates scope and value clearly<br>- Supports approval decision<br>- Aligns stakeholders on next steps |
