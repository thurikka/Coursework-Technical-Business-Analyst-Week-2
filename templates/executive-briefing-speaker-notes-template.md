# Executive Briefing Speaker Notes Template

Use this file to keep your slide narration concise and executive-friendly.

## Slide 1 - Recommendation
- What decision are you asking for?
- Why this Phase 1, now?

## Slide 2 - Why now
- What is the scale of the current problem?
- Which operational and financial consequences matter most?

## Slide 3 - Discovery evidence
- Which 2-3 findings matter most?
- What changed because of those findings?

## Slide 4 - Value case
- Which opportunities rank highest and why?
- Where are assumptions strongest and weakest?

## Slide 5 - Scope and exclusions
- What are you deliberately not trying to solve yet?
- Why does that make the recommendation more credible?

## Slide 6 - Workflow and prototype
- What does the main journey do?
- How are exceptions handled?

## Slide 7 - Delivery readiness
- How many stories exist?
- What are the key dependencies?
- Why is the backlog usable by a delivery team?

## Slide 8 - Change risk
- What could undermine adoption?
- Which mitigations are already reflected in scope or backlog?

## Slide 9 - Ask
- What do you want leadership to approve?
- What happens next if they agree?
